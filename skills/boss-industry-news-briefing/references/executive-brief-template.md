# Executive Brief Template

Use one of these templates and keep the answer concise.

## Template A: Flash Alert

### Key event

- One sentence on what happened, with the exact date.

### Why it matters

- 2 to 4 bullets on revenue, cost, competition, regulation, product, or supply-chain impact.

### What to watch next

- 2 to 3 bullets on the next milestone, likely risk, or decision trigger.

### Sources

- List the core source links.

## Template B: Daily Boss Brief

### Executive summary

- 3 to 5 bullets with the most important changes first.

### China

- Key development
- Business implication
- Suggested watchpoint

### Global

- Key development
- Business implication
- Suggested watchpoint

### Cross-border or strategic implication

- One short section connecting the domestic and global developments to a management takeaway.

### Recommended actions

- 2 to 5 concrete actions or watch items for leadership.

### Sources

- Group links by item.

## Template C: Weekly Executive Scan

### This week's biggest shifts

- Summarize the 3 to 5 changes that matter most.

### Themes

- Policy and regulation
- Competition and capital
- Demand, pricing, and supply chain
- Technology and product milestones

### What changed vs last week

- Mention deltas only when the comparison period is known.

### Leadership takeaways

- State what management should decide, monitor, or prepare for.

### Sources

- Keep the source list compact and traceable.
