# Blended Training Program Patterns

Use this reference when the client wants a recurring online training rhythm plus periodic offline workshops.

Typical request examples:

- 每月一期线上课，每次2小时
- 每季度一期线下课，每次2天
- 线上打基础，线下做实操

## Best-practice design

Use a layered design:

1. `线上`: build common tool literacy and lightweight habits
2. `线下`: do deeper practical drills and business application
3. `考核`: use small tests online and practical assignments offline
4. `激励`: set visible recognition to sustain adoption

## Recommended proposal structure

1. 方案背景
2. 培训目标
3. 培训体系设计
4. 线上课程安排
5. 线下课程安排
6. 实施保障
7. 预期效果

## Recommended online design

Monthly online classes should be:

- short
- simple
- tool-first
- immediately usable

Recommended fields:

| 期次 | 频率 | 时长 | 培训主题 | 重点内容 | 适合对象 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 第1期 | 每月1次 | 2小时 | AI基础工具入门 | 通用AI工具、提示词基础、办公常见场景 | 全员 |

Good online themes:

- AI基础工具入门
- 提示词与高效提问
- AI办公自动化
- AI宣传文案与海报辅助
- AI会议纪要与PPT制作
- AI知识库与常见问答

## Recommended offline design

Quarterly offline workshops should be:

- scenario-based
- hands-on
- cross-tool
- output-oriented

Recommended fields:

| 期次 | 频率 | 时长 | 培训模块 | 重点内容 | 关键产出 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 第1期 | 每季度1次 | 2天 | AI办公与宣传实操 | 文档、表格、PPT、宣传内容、视频脚本、知识库基础 | 部门实操作业 |

Good offline focus:

- 办公自动化
- 宣传内容生产
- 客服与问答标准化
- 数据整理与报表
- 岗位场景实操

## Quality upgrades

To turn a 75-point blended scheme into a stronger one:

- connect online themes to offline practice explicitly
- show what each monthly class solves
- show what each quarterly workshop outputs
- separate `基础普及` from `业务实操`
- make the progression visible across the year

## Notes

- Do not make online classes too heavy.
- Do not repeat the same content in every month.
- Use online for普及 and offline for强化.
- If the customer is a large group, mention internal讲师培养 or AI骨干机制 when helpful.
- Do not replace tables with placeholders. If you mention an online or offline curriculum table, show the rows directly.
