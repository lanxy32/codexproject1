# Industry AI Training Patterns

Use this reference when the boss gives a client name, client industry, and a short business brief, then asks for an `AI + 客户行业` training scheme.

This reference is especially useful for:

- one-day business training
- two-day practical bootcamps
- clients asking for `案例demo`
- mixed-channel businesses that combine traditional channels and new media

## Working intake model

Start from this input order when available:

1. 客户名称
2. 客户行业
3. 客户相关信息
4. 培训时长
5. 参考图片或课程框架
6. 关键需求词 such as `案例`, `demo`, `业务培训`, `实战`, `落地`

If the images are already present in the thread, use them as structural references. Do not ask the user to resend them unless they are missing or unclear.

## How to make the scheme feel industry-native

A weak version says:

- AI can help with content
- AI can help with customer service
- AI can help with efficiency

A stronger version says:

- which products or services the demos use
- which channels are being optimized
- which compliance constraints matter
- which specific deliverables the trainees will produce

## One-day scheme pattern

For a one-day industry training, the most reliable shape is:

1. AI认知与行业应用全景
2. 提示词与行业合规表达
3. 内容或业务素材生产
4. 视频/渠道/客户服务/知识库其中一到两个核心链路
5. 全链路串联与短期落地清单

Recommended time logic:

| 序号 | 时间 | 模块 | 目标 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 09:00-09:30 | 行业认知 | 明确AI在该行业的价值 |
| 2 | 09:30-10:30 | 提示词与规则 | 学会高质量、合规的输入 |
| 3 | 10:40-12:00 | 内容或业务生成 | 完成首轮行业实操 |
| 4 | 14:00-15:30 | 渠道/视频/服务专项 | 解决一个核心业务链路 |
| 5 | 15:40-16:40 | 知识库/客服/流程 | 沉淀组织能力 |
| 6 | 16:40-17:30 | 全链路复盘与行动计划 | 明确培训后的动作 |

## E-commerce standard-product pattern

For standard-product e-commerce clients, especially supplements, food, daily goods, or health-adjacent categories, emphasize:

- content efficiency
- multi-channel adaptation
- customer service standardization
- compliant expression
- repurchase and private-domain follow-up

Recommended modules:

1. AI认知与行业全景
2. 提示词工程与合规写法
3. 内容库搭建与多渠道生产
4. 视频运营与流量放大
5. 智能客服与企业知识库
6. 全链路实操与阶段落地

## Demo design rules

If the client asks for `案例demo`, add demos across modules, not only at the end.

Good demo distribution:

- Module 1: 同行业AI内容案例展示
- Module 2: 行业合规提示词生成演示
- Module 3: 从选题到文案/图片/详情页的生成演示
- Module 4: 短视频 or 流量优化演示
- Module 5: 客服问答 or 知识库演示
- Module 6: 全链路串联演示

## Compliance-sensitive categories

For supplements, food, health, and adjacent regulated categories:

- Avoid overclaiming efficacy
- Avoid using prohibited or risky benefit wording as if it were approved
- Teach compliant wording templates
- Add one explicit compliance reminder module or subsection

Do not provide legal advice. Frame this as content risk awareness and communication standardization.

## What improves a 70-point scheme to a 90-point scheme

Upgrade in these ways:

- move from broad blocks to exact time slots
- move from topic names to module goals
- move from one generic demo to module-by-module demos
- move from tool list to business-chain integration
- move from generic ending to `1-3个月落地动作`
- move from only teaching to `实训任务 + 输出成果`

## End-of-day output ideas

Choose outputs that match the customer:

- 行业内容提示词包
- 选题库或内容库初稿
- 短视频脚本初稿
- 电商详情页文案初稿
- 客服知识库问答初稿
- 1-3个月AI落地行动清单

## Apply carefully

- Keep tools optional and secondary to the business problem.
- Match the demo to the customer's revenue scale and team maturity.
- If the client is still very early, do not overload with advanced agent building unless explicitly requested.
